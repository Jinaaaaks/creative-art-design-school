We cap every cohort at twelve students. This is deliberate — we believe meaningful critique and instructor attention require a small group. It also means spaces are limited and fill up quickly each intake.
Most courses use Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, After Effects). A subscription to Creative Cloud is recommended. Some courses also use Figma, which is free. We provide full software lists after acceptance.
Yes. Online courses follow the same curriculum, same faculty, and same critique structure as on-site courses. Sessions are live, not pre-recorded. The only difference is you are not in the same room as your cohort — though many online students tell us the asynchronous flexibility suits them well.
